Our story

The Genesis

At Prehome, our journey began with a deep-seated conviction that the dream of owning a home should be more than just a distant aspiration for first-time homebuyers. Witnessing the trials and tribulations of these individuals, we embarked on a mission to reimagine this journey and make it not only achievable but transformative.
In the heart of our vision lies the acknowledgment of the hurdles that first-time homebuyers encounter from the opaque system built on distrust, to the formidable down payment requirements and adding to it the intricate process of finding a home that truly feels like their own. It was this recognition that fuelled the inception of our revolutionary and empowering rent-to-own program.

Our rent-to-own program is more than a transaction; it's a bridge to the dream of home ownership.

The Reimagination

Our rent-to-own program is more than a transaction; it's a bridge to the dream of home ownership. We recognized that saving for a down payment while paying rent elsewhere is a substantial hurdle. Thus, we innovatively designed a program that allows individuals to accumulate savings while already living in the home they aspire to own.

The Unique Proposition

In our commitment to providing stability and peace of mind, we chose to exclusively offer ready-to-move-in homes. This eliminates the uncertainty of construction timelines and quality variations, allowing our clients to step into their dream homes immediately.
Furthermore, our unique program empowers our clients at every step. Individuals not only gain access to high-quality homes but also receive the valuable benefit of time. While enjoying the comfort of their chosen residence, they also have the opportunity to accumulate savings specifically designated for their future down payment.
But our innovation doesn't stop there. Understanding the dynamic nature of the real estate market, we introduced a groundbreaking feature - locking in the price. By securing the home at a fixed price during the rental period, we provide our clients with a unique sense of control and stability.

The Transformative Experience

For our first-time homebuyers, the journey begins not with blueprints and construction timelines, but with stepping into a home that is already theirs. It's a transformative experience, not just in terms of building equity and savings but in the immediacy of calling a ready-to-move-in house their home.

The Impact

Our startup is not just about redefining home ownership; it's about reshaping lives. It's about turning what once felt like an insurmountable challenge into an empowering journey of growth, stability, and accomplishment.
In every ready to move in home, we see the seed of ownership taking roots. In every locked-in price, we witness the promise of a stable future. And in every first-time homebuyer, we see a success story waiting to be told.
